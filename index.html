FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with me and how I can help your book succeed.

What exactly does a book marketing expert do?

+

A book marketing expert creates and executes strategies designed specifically to increase book sales and author visibility. This includes Amazon optimization, advertising, social media, email marketing, PR, book launches, and brand development. I focus exclusively on books, which gives me industry knowledge that general marketers cannot match.

What genres do you work with?

+

All genres. Fiction including romance, thriller, mystery, sci-fi, fantasy, literary fiction, and YA. Non-fiction including business, self-help, memoir, health, and leadership. Also children's books, poetry, academic works, and more. Each genre has its own audience behaviors and I tailor my approach accordingly.

Do you work with self-published authors?

+

Yes. I work with self-published, traditionally published, and hybrid authors. The core marketing principles are similar, but the tactics differ based on your publishing model. I adapt my approach to your specific situation.

Is it too early to start marketing before publication?

+

Not at all. The ideal time to start is three to six months before publication. Building your brand, growing your email list, and creating pre-launch buzz early gives your book the strongest possible start.

My book has been out and sales are slow. Can you help?

+

Absolutely. Many of my most successful campaigns have been with authors who came to me months or years after publication. It is never too late. We analyze what has been done, find opportunities, and create a plan to reignite your sales.

How much do your services cost?

+

Pricing varies based on scope and services required. I work with various budget levels and always find a solution that fits. The best way to get accurate pricing is to schedule a free strategy session.

How long before I see results?

+

Most authors see measurable improvements within 30 to 60 days. Significant results typically emerge within 60 to 90 days. Sustained marketing generates compounding returns over 6 to 12 months.

Do you guarantee bestseller status?

+

No. Be cautious of anyone who does. What I guarantee is a professional, data-driven approach that maximizes your book's chances, along with transparent communication and genuine commitment to your goals.

Do you work with international authors?

+

Yes. I work with authors worldwide. All consultations and campaign management are conducted remotely, and I accommodate different time zones.
